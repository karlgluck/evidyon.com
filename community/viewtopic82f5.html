New Version is OUT!

by Karl G. » Sat Sep 05, 2009 11:25 pm

Hey everyone,

There is a brand new Evidyon out ... right now! I'm crossing my fingers and hoping its stable because I'm going to bed. I tested it and it seems fine to me.

You'll need to re-download the installer--the auto-updater will get the update, but it won't connect to the new server. Be sure to uninstall your old version first.

http://www.unseenstudios.com/evidyon/do ... vidyon.msi


New stuff includes:
- New kinds of monsters
- Geosids now have "Guardians" which will attack anyone not in the guild--so a Geosid is harder to take over, and once you take it you have someone on your side. To take over a Geosid, you have to kill the guardian. When the guardian respawns, the guild with the greatest # of players nearby will get the Geosid.
- fixed trans bug
- fixed some other random issues
- moved us to a new server!
