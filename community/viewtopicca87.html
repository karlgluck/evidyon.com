by Vitriol » Sun Jul 29, 2007 10:31 pm

After the basic walk around and communicate part, I would like to see things added in the following order.
1. Implement the stat system. This includes playable races, classes, skills, hp mp, etc. Even if you cant DO anything with it. (like level up) All characters should have their stats "frozen" until the game is ready to actually play.
2. Add basic non-aggresive NPS. Merchants mostly. (we prolly shouldnt even worry about guards for a while) The ability to interact (sell/buy) is essential. Even if there is nothing to buy or sell.
3. Add items. we only need items up to a certain point at first, we can add stuff.
4. Add monsters. Again, we only need a few at first.
By this time we have the whole newbie island together and the game is essentially ready to play.
5. finish mapping the main world, unfreeze the stats, and have a general release. beta 1.
