I was modeling a wooden shield the other day and i thought "why can't PV items have animations like xenimus has?"
So i made a couple mockups of simple weapons animations. Be aware these are very crude because they are just demos of what is possible (anything)

Heres a sword that has two balls traveling up the blade on either side:
http://projectvolucris.com/video/play.php?vid=81

(kind of hard to see because uploading destroyed the video quality)

and heres a staff that has a ball floating around the tip (something similiar could be done to characters for a sort of divinus effect)
http://projectvolucris.com/video/play.php?vid=82

remember that these are just simple animations. much more complex ones are certainly within reach, and I'd like some ideas from the community
