by Joe M. » Sun May 10, 2009 2:14 pm

I'm assuming that you are indeed running Vista?

The permissions are tricky with Vista, and don't yet play nice with Evidyon (this will be included in a bug-fixing update in the next couple weeks, which should also fix the weird Vista graphics bug--the reason all the models are messed up).

The easiest way to fix this is to move the game directory to your desktop or documents folder or some such. You can also mess with the folder permissions if you like, but it's weird; I got mine to work more out of blind luck than anything else.
