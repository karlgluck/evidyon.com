by Karl G. » Tue Sep 04, 2007 5:14 pm

"good" is very subjective; the best texture artists can make 250 poly models look incredible, and bad modelers can use 150000 polys, smoothing, normal, bump and texture maps with per-pixel lighting and still end up with a heaping pile of poo. But a "good" quality character model for this game should likely be around 2000-3000 polys.
