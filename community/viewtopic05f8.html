Wednesday 8/27 Update - Small download!

by Unseen Studios » Wed Aug 27, 2008 3:25 pm

Only the client executable and config.ini need to be updated:

http://www.unseenstudios.com/evidyon/do ... vidyon.exe
http://www.unseenstudios.com/evidyon/do ... config.ini

New features:
  • FPS cap on every screen - configure it in config.ini! Slower computers can use a 30 or even down to 5 FPS limit
  • Turn off f7 under [Debugging] in config.ini to get rid of the fog (I toned it down, but this should completely fix that blue-ing out issue)
  • Controls should be more responsive now
  • Version control! You'll be notified automatically when a new download is available
  • You can see how many people are logged in on the main screen
