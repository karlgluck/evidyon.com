by Karl G. » Wed Jul 30, 2008 7:00 pm

I'm right on target for a mid-August alpha--it'll be close, but I should be able to make it.

Check out the latest video (new, just released!)

http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Rd5I1idZnKc

There's a LOT going on here:
- character animations
- dying and respawning
- serverside stat-based HP/MP regeneration
- characters collide with each other when alive
- projectiles collide
- combos (only 1 in this video, fireball + freezing aura)
- chat

Stuff that also works that isn't in the video because I made it really fast:
- sweet lookin' HP/MP bars that don't look like doofy blocks
- water effects and varying land-heights
- items that you can drop, pickup and equip

Stuff that's coming soon (will start work this weekend):
- class-based spells and a spell list
- monster spawning / AI control of monsters
