Re: regarding models and animation

by Karl G. » Mon Sep 29, 2008 5:44 pm

The short answer: yes, in the future.

The slightly longer answer is that it would be annoying to implement right at this moment, and that the current system does support animated *textures*. You can achieve the rotating ball effect by animating the ball's texture, for example. Items could also have special particle effects attached to them (like dirus stuff does)
