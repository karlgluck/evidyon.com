Update on the Questing System

by Karl G. » Tue Jun 02, 2009 3:41 pm

For the past 5 days or so I've been hammering away at a questing system. Although I've mentioned my progress to several people, I wanted to make a post to fill everyone in on the details.

As of this afternoon, I have created and successfully tested Evidyon's first basic quest. There are dozens of different quest types and not all of the different ways of completing them have been tested (this is something you'll all be helping me with!) However, the limited features I've tested work smoothly enough that it just requires a bit more tweaking before I can put out an update for you all to try. Expect something in the very near future.
