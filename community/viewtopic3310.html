Re: Evidyon Alpha 2 Bugs and Issues

by Karl G. » Sat Jan 24, 2009 4:48 pm

ubern00ber wrote:Movement is still very buggy... It still seems to be directive as before(which then causes you to walk away in funny directions if you lag out) and often when I cast spells and stuff I'm not in the place that I appear to be.. and well things jus get plain ol' weird from there.


From a coding perspective, movement has absolutely nothing in common with the old method. The weirdness from spells is fixed for next update (it was a server issue), as is what I think is causing your problem with lag. The client optimizes your movement to smooth out jitter in the server's packet stream. One of the approximations makes you move slightly less than you should under big lag spikes, but that's been fixed.

Another thing that I changed for next update is that I prevent "glancing" collisions from registering: i.e. if you try to move past a creature, it fudges your collision circle a little. This has made movement significantly more intuitive.
