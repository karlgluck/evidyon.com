I've had this same frustration. I'm not sure what a good solution might be, though.

Perhaps a simple solution might be this:
A character has in his inventory a consumable which varies in degree, let's say it's a Large Red Potion.
There appears in his Use menu "Large Red Potion," which he can key.
In addition, there appear two new options: "Red Potion (All): Large->Small" and "Red Potion (All): Small->Large."


This might be too cluttered, but it's the first way which occurred to me by which the obvious pitfalls might be avoided.
