October 18 - 3.7 Is Out

by Karl G.
Evidyon Chronicles
The first souls lost during the cataclysmic world-breaking Evidyon have begun their return. With their help, the Geosids reclaim power from items and have pushed back the Chaos surrounding Nexus. As it grew stronger, one Geosid has achieved new links with more distant pieces of the fractured world.


3.7 contains:
  • Quickness/Haste/Rancor repairs
  • Casting spells while attacking fix
  • Spell MP cost, damage and delay adjustment
  • Spreads on hunter spells that should have them
  • Boosted base walk speed
  • If you die but are wearing a valtus mederi charm, then get disconnected or die you will not lose XP (you'll be respawned instead)
  • If you want to change the fonts in Evidyon, you can go into the config file and change them to anything you want.
  • (surprises!)

Some people have been asking, so I wanted to say officially: there will be a stat reset after all balancing is finished.
