Alright, guys, update on the update.

It should be ready by Friday, and will include:
  • Items: rock-solid system, working anti-duping mechanism, no more random disappearances. (Tip for the meantime: if you die, immediately log off and log back on, which should prevent a certain bug which from time to time causes items to poof on death).
  • Server will back up more regularly and more reliably, which will prevent item/exp loss from crashes.
  • Spells: casting delay will be much lower, queuing will work properly (i.e., if you cast a spell and then in the middle of casting cast another, the new one won't be canceled, as it is currently, but will be queued and cast once the first spell is finished), more damage.
  • New HP/MP equations which should help out a lot with balancing.
  • Party system: larger "/join" radius, shared experience.
