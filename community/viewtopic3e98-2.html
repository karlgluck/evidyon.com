You original complaint:

Your proposed solution:

The only reason that I can see you give as to why an actual EXP loss would be preferable to penalty EXP is:

But that would seem to work directly contrary to fixing your original complaint!

It seems that a better answer to your original complaint would be some combination of: (a) no penalty EXP until a couple deaths; (b) a cap on penalty EXP.

Of course if you want to discuss penalty-EXP v. EXP-loss, that's another question. We would have to reframe the question and focus strictly on why one might be better than the other.
