I know this is probably a long shot and i hope this doesnt go down to bad, i spent alot of time working this out and actually thinking about the arena itself.

Basically it is the simple team battle game we hae all played any enjoyed, flag capture. I'm sure your wondering how does this work in xenimus, well let me explain.

Obviously you have 2 sides, There will be a minimum of 3 players each side to a maximum of 6 players each side each event. Now once we have 6 players join the arena, they are all asigned to a team (randomly if possible) so now we have an equally chosen 3v3.
