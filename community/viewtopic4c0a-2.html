So here's what's going on: there were a couple bugs in the world manager that were causing weird stuff, one of them I haven't figured out yet. After running for more than a couple of hours, about half of the world's spawned monsters become "zombies": they are present in the world, but they don't move, die or interact with the rest of the world at all. As much as I want this thing to go out now, I think that putting out something half-assed and semifunctional is worse than delaying it until the end of this week. I should know by now that just because I set a date doesn't mean that's how it's going to happen

I promise to keep you all posted. I am traveling back to Ithaca tomorrow, but will be working between packing and doing laundry today and while on the plane.
