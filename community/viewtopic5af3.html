Posted: Thu Jan 25, 2007 10:29 am
by Karl G.
Based on your input from the poll and talking to various people, unless EJ responds to my proposal, I am going forward with creating all of our own content. I am hoping that this community can create all of the static models for the game, and that we can purchase the animated ones from a contractor. Out of the dozen or so that have contacted me, none have followed through with their promises of example meshes and I don't want to work with someone who I have to chase around to get anything from, especially if they're being paid. I am going to try two more modelers and hopefully be able to get some cheap, low-poly animated character models from them. We need some basic content at this point so here's what we need to get this game started!

Guidelines
When you create content, post it on this forum with "[Type] - Name" as the subject. For example "[Texture] - Stones", "[Model] - Tree"

Textures
All textures should use PURE BLACK (RGB: 0,0,0) as their transparent color). These textures should be able to replace the Xenimus textures. To test how they look in the game, save over copies in Xenimus and play the game or use the map editor. Don't bother replacing character textures, as they won't be used. Don't bother replacing the "tx#" textures. They weren't in the original xenimus and when we need the extension we can make our own.
