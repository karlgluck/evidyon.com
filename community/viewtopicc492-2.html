by Karl G. » Sun Feb 11, 2007 6:33 pm

Wow that looks great! By the way, for those of you that want to make items: make sure the handle (where you want the player's hand to be) is located at the origin, coordinates 0,0,0. An easy way to test this is when you rotate the model, it should spin around the handle. For the shields, make the origin where you want the player's arm to be--remember it's the left arm, too!
