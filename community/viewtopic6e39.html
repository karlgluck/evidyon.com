September 6, 2008 - Version 1.6.0, 1.7.0 - brand new map!!

by Karl G. » Sat Sep 06, 2008 11:39 am

Okay everyone!! It's out!

Version 1.6.0: http://www.unseenstudios.com/evidyon/download/evidyon-setup.msi
Patch to 1.7.0: http://www.unseenstudios.com/evidyon/download/evidyon.exe (download and put in C:\Program Files\Evidyon)
Note: 1.7.1 changed item drop method. No download is necessary.

BE SURE TO /RESPAWN WHEN YOU LOG IN!


Version 1.6.0 has a whole bunch of updates:
  • Type "-" in front of text to GLOBAL CHAT
  • The world is HUGE!
  • Monsters now attack intelligently and don't get stuck as frequently
  • The middle of the circle of bricks at the spawning area is a safe zone where damage is reflected
  • Melee classes now have an area of effect spell
  • There are monsters to hunt from level 2 to level 40
  • All items are now dropped by monsters
  • Monster strength & exp significantly adjusted
  • Zoomed out the camera
  • I spent a few hours and came up with a pretty good looking "run" animation
  • Shields look a lot more pimp now
  • There are mountains in the world and a new kind of blackened terrain
  • Probably more stuff that I'm missing


Version 1.7.0 fixes:
  • Drop items on death--yes, all of them (for now)
  • as per a request I received in-game, "strike" has a greater range and width
  • Global chat is fixed

Version 1.7.1 fixes:
  • Drop only inventory/randomly 1 item on death. It was too hard to hunt otherwise.

I worked 6 PM - 3 AM last night and 7 AM until now (1 pm) on this...so I'm probably missing some changes that you'll just have to figure out


Would someone mind mirroring this download?

Karl G.
