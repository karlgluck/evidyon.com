If you're new, check out the basic instructions.


In this update:
  • Upped the power of knights
  • Beam is now in the game! No, not in its final form. I just added the effect to "mortalitas" so that I could see how it looked. You'll get a much better one
  • EXP sharing reduced to players within 3 levels of each other
  • + Speed now gone from most items. Players were just too dang fast. Knights without teleport may have a bit of trouble getting around quickly until we release their attack-run & faster movement spells.
  • Tweaked "vortex", hopefully it will behave now. nope... :/
