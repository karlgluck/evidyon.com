Guidelines

-At current, Project Volucris needs files that serve same or similar purposes as those described below. Files that serve purposes that were not incorporated into the original xenimus are welcome but are unlikely to be incorporated, or seriously discussed, until later stages of the game.
-Unoriginal material that is royalty free may be submitted, please do not otherwise plagiarize.
-Whenever possible please use .ogg format.
-If you cannot read .ogg files, try downloading vlc media player here or media player classic here
