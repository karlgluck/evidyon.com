My opinions thus far

Posted: Wed Oct 15, 2008 7:44 pm
by a_link_to_the_past
Granted I know this game isn't ready for public release and still needs a lot of work.. However I figured I would throw my two cents out there just for reference.

Visual

+ The rich colors make the game feel alive..
+ I really like the appearance of the water..
+ I dig the sexy black wife beater that my character has on.

- Weapons and Armors seem to kind of float around the characters rather than being connected to them.
- Some sheilds seem rather large in relation to the characters
- Walking animation could use some work... he reminds me of quazi motto when he walks around.
- Spin doesn't spin.

Gameplay

+ Pretty smooth and without lag outs
+ Challenging yet not impossible game play.

- Spawn likes to get bunched up around the water's edge and cliffs. Don't get me wrong.. I love easy kills but its something that needs work.
- Spin seems to make baddies run away when I hit them with it while moving.
- Walking around water as well as cliffs is a little bit difficult and may be why enemies get hung up around those areas.
- Adding additional armor didn't seem to help my defense too much.

Okay... theres a small list of good things and things that could use work, based upon playing this latest release.
