Woah now. I was ignoring your other post for these reasons, and I'm locking this one for the same.

- you haven't played this version
- you have no idea how it is going to be implemented
- the way you suggested to "change" it was actually a bad way of implementing what I already have in mind

Enchantments are not just a percentage; the spell you get at level 1 to add 10% hp (if one existed) won't give you 10% hp at level 150. It adds 10% until level 8 or so. Let's say that is 50 HP. After that, casting that spell will never give you more than 50 hp. To get more hp you have to use the next version, which costs more mp, looks different and gives you 10% increase in hp for levels 10-30.

As I said, for now no enchantment has much of an effect at all.
