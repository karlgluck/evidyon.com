Re: Stats Explanation

by Joelshup » Mon Nov 02, 2009 9:39 pm

Strength = Determines how hard you hit with melee or ranged attacks, not confirmed but I think it increases HP just a bit.
Agility = Determines how fast your will hit with melee, ranged, and how fast you can cast spells.
Constitution = Increase the amount of HP the player has.
Intelligence = Increase the amount of MP the player has as well as increasing spell damage.
Wisdom = Has been described in other posts, I will try my best at this one. Increase MP ever slightly as well as increase the buff duration of your spells and I think possibly increases spell damage, but I am unsure about that this.

I hope that helps and do correct me if I am wrong, especially on Wisdom.
