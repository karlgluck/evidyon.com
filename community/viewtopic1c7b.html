by Joe M.
I had a very similar problem a few months ago. The problem for me was the User Account Control; once I turned it off the problem went away.
So if you have it turned on, turn it off and then try running Evidyon.

If you don't know how to turn UAC off:
1. Open the Control Panel.
2. Find the "User Accounts" screen. To do this:
......(a) in the standard Vista interface, click "User Accounts and Family Safety", then "User Accounts";
......(b) in the "Classic View", click "User Accounts".
3. Click the link at the bottom labeled "Turn User Account Control on or off."
4. Uncheck the box and click "OK".
