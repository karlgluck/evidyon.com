by Karl G. » Tue May 12, 2009 10:04 am

  • Button sounds
  • Walking sounds

  • Various bug-fixes

Rolling out another quick update

by Karl G. » Tue May 12, 2009 3:24 pm

Added features: right-click on a stacked item in inventory to split the stack. Click on an item to re-stack it with the first of its type in the inventory. Items are now consumed last-to-first in inventory order so that smaller stacks are consumed before taking members of larger ones.

Made 'vitalize' now consume Geonite equal to your *current* hp and mp added together. This way, if your HP and MP are both low it makes it easier to use, but spamming the button to stay maxed on stats remains very expensive.
