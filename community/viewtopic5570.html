Re: Mini-update: Fixed buff spells

by Joe M. » Wed Mar 25, 2009 1:13 am

With the damage system, it should (oddly) become easier to balance once we have more monsters and other content as reference. So the real fine-tuning will have to wait for Beta: anything we "fix" now will probably be discovered to be broken once we have a more varied world in which to play. I think the damage should be good enough to work with as it is for now (it seems that way to me--but then again I like the low damage). But we'll definitely be putting a *lot* of thought into getting this just right once we're in Beta. In fact, considerations such as damage balancing will be one of the primary purposes of Beta (the other being adding content).
