by Draco
My vote is "Ciphered Language with description"

I think the names should be ciphered, but still easy to understand/simplified. I found a site for multiple methods of translation of specific words from English to Latin.

There's another aspect that plays a role in spell names for games... the abbreviations! When it comes to the appeal of the game, how things are abbreviated actually does have an affect on how players feel the game. This is also why keeping things simple is appealing, players can relate and share the same agreed abbreviations they like... I'm just bringing this mental aspect to your attention to help choose spell names.

I like the idea of the NPC's who inform you of the spells you learn.

This site and the words seem to match up perfectly with a great feel of the words to match a good feel from in the game, Here are some examples, the words, and what they mean in english. I'll put probable abbreviations/nicknames in quotes, and Xen Equivalents as "XE".

Examples:

Socius Rememdium: ally heal - This is an example of a bad abbreviation... it's horrific looking, doesn't sound too good--at least not in my opinion--and doesn't match a good abbreviation... so perhaps a different pair of words would be better such as this next one...

Curatus Comitis: Heal Friend/Comrade "CC / group heal / comitis"
XE: none really, except heal all.

Curatus: heal "heal / cure / cura / curatus"
XE: mederi

Magus Fulsi - Magical Flash/Shine/Beam. "MF / Fulsi / Magus"
XE: could be beam, or wiz bomb, ect.

Grandire Alacritas: Increase Quickness "GA / alac"
XE: velocitas

Grandire Validus: Increase Strength/Power/Vigor "GV / vali/ valid"
XE: robur

Grandire Totus: Increase Entire/All "GT / totus / multi buff"
This could be used for pretty much the old all-buff spell.
XE: Multi Animagicus (I Think)

Lux Lucis Fulsi: Light Beam/Flash "LLF, beam, fulsi"
XE: Lunae Lumen Nex, moonbeam.

Vigoratus: Stout, Hearty... could be used as an HP Regen spell "vigor"
XE: none

Curatus Valde: Heal Very Much / Heal Greatly "CV or probably same as curatus"
XE: Vis Mederi / high lvl mederi

Magus Telum: Magical Arrow/Dart, could be a spell for ranged-weapons.
XE: none

Incendere: set fire to/burn/scorch "incend / fire"
XE: flamma

Magus Glacies/Glacialis: Magical Ice. "glacies / ice"
XE: Freeze

Persultare Trans: Leap/Jump across/over. "trans"
XE: Pergere Trans
