Speeding up the characters would help a lot (to cover ground and to balance char speed with monster speed--right now I can't catch up to the baddies when they start to run away), but the basic problem is that there are huge tracts of land with nothing in them. A smaller, more populated area for low levels with weaker monsters is desperately needed.
