October 13 - Another Quick Update, and Recruiting!

by Karl G. » Tue Oct 13, 2009 3:46 pm

First: I just uploaded a quick feature-addition. Right-clicking will now re-cast the last targeted spell you used. For example, if you teleport then right-click, it will cast teleport again. This does not work for items or for spells where you don't have to pick a target. Enjoy, button-mashers =)

Second: recruit more players! Playing is always more fun with a bigger crowd. I'd love to see some new faces around here--so much so that the next time I see more than 20 players online, everyone will get a Holy Figurine. More than 30? Expect something even better!

Here are some things you could try:
- If you have cool screenshots, upload them to tinypic or some other service and show them to your friends or post them on appropriate forums
- Upload videos of Evidyon to YouTube & share them
- Add Evidyon to your signature on other forums
- Make an Evidyon T-shirt
- Play Evidyon in public places and show it off! (I've had people come ask me what I was playing in earlier versions of the game)
- Make a website on a free server that shows off your screenshots, videos, avatar stories, etc.
- Link to evidyon.com!

By doing some of these things, you can help make Evidyon a really great game that will just keep growing!

If you have any other ideas, post 'em here.


~Karl

Edit 1: The Earthen Emblem award went out last night! Congrats everyone, check the screenshots on the main website for pictures!

Karl G.
