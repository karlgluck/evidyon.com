by thadiusofx3 » Sun Jun 22, 2008 4:13 pm

I haven't forgotten about the project. I've been lurking, but I have refrained from posting for about a decade now.

However, I'm still very much alive and still involved in the project. Ive come across several tools in the past few months to develop some texture and models, and I've been honing my skills.

Single-handedly modeling every single environment object in xenimus for the map maker kind of burned me out so i stopped modeling for awhile. (http://www.projectvolucris.com/files/3d ... /Finished/)

But now im back with a brand new computer and ready to model again. I started off with a simple hammer, wooden handle, stone head.



Im going to move over to staves next. If anyone has any requests for models of any kind (weapons, shields, armor, scenery, etc) lemme know and ill get it done.
