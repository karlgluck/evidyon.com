Hey Marz, yes there are more items for you to find. You've got a lot of the higher level ones, but you're missing the rest of the crystalline set, the sleight of hand, the lion's heart breastplate, iridescent cape and a few others. These very high level items have approximately a 1/320000 chance of dropping.

The problem is that you've managed to get to a level beyond where I've designed things to do. There isn't really anything to do at level 25 because I didn't expect anyone to actually GET to level 25. That's why level 26 takes 10 million experience--it's a barrier until I get a chance to do more design.

Next update there should be more, but it'll be mostly mid-level content, new spells, etc. If you can suggest something that you might want to see, let me know and I'll try to work it in as well.
