Posted: Fri Jun 22, 2007 8:12 pm
by Ender
Hey Everyone,

For any semi-new people here, SilverT and I have done most of the music / sounds so far, but haven't been around for the past couple of months. I don't know what SilverT's plans are, but I'm back and am ready to start cranking stuff out. (plus I switched my major from engineering to music, so I've learned some new tricks).

Anyway, I was reading Links post in the audio forum about how a good game has music which matches its dungeons. While a separate theme for every hunting area in an MMO isn't practical, I think it is definitely a good idea to capture the general feel of the game in the music.

So here is my proposition to mapmakers:

You tell me what defining aspects or themes your map/dungeon has, what a journey through it would be like, etc., and what you imagine the music being like for that area.... then I'll try and compose it! In addition to making the game a whole lot cooler, this will make my job a lot easier since I'll have a more clearly defined idea of what I'm going for.

Let me know what you think,

Zach
