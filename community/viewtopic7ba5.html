I've been talking to a 3d modeler sent over by Serenity (thank you!!) and it sounds like he's what we need. He will do textured, non-animated models for FREE and seems very willing to work for a small budget. He is knowledgeable and professional. But we have a little problem.

I felt kinda dumb saying our budget was only $15.31. I told him that I hoped we could arrange something, but I'd like to be able to talk to him with a real offer next time, so I'd like to start our first official round of donations.   • All donations will be completely used to fund the development and purchase of 3D models
  • Donations made now will be applied as game-credits in the future when the game completes
