Version 2.3 - Sunday February 1

by Karl G. » Sun Feb 01, 2009 3:59 pm

I rebalanced knights and sorcerers, and adjusted the difficulty of monsters in the world. Starting a character is tons more fun now, and mages actually have a chance

Fixes include:
- exp adjustment
- hp/mp adjustment
- spell strengths adjusted
- animation bugs resolved
- trees are solid now
- you shouldn't be able to pass through solid areas anymore
- rarer items drop a little more frequently
- some items look better! (still working on this)

I don't want to get ahead of myself, but I think I'm approaching the "sweet spot" for leveling difficulty & fun, keeping things fluid but challenging. I played for almost an hour before releasing this thing
