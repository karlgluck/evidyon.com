Posted: Sat May 30, 2009 3:24 am
by marzuku
The idea came when talking of making a monument for the Alpha testers instead of a tombstone(which sounded boring).

To Quote Rapey, Xlpicit:

Imagine 3 years from now when players go do this quest and can actually see back into the past how the game used to look like.
The new players would be wearing flashy armors and sick looking weapons and figthing "The Alpha Testers" known as "The Ancients" Wearing aurelian armors spirit dragon axes, wands of starlight etc.

and i thought, how awsome isnt that? creating a quest and a monument at the same time, and providing insight in what the game used to look like, also we would be remembered forever.

Now the Quest:

Questline: Facing the Ancients

In the begining there was only darkness and chaos, then there was Geona.
Geona supported several life forms, some cruel and malicious, some good and righteous but there was still chaos.
There were few who would oppose chaos, These few were named the Ancients, they were called so because they were the first of their kind, the first Heroes to explore Geona and fight of chaos for the good of the land.
Chaos however grew stronger, and as Chaos grew, the Ancients weakened by it's overwhelming force.
Wielding weapons forged by flames from chaos itself, one by one the Ancients turned and became the very thing they had opposed, and the world once again grew dark.

Young Knight/sorceror/Class
Would you let an old man see the light once again before he wanders into the next path of life?

Put to rest the men once known as "The Ancients"

And what the new players would have to do is enter some kind of catacomb where there was a room filled with Alpha player copies as skeletons, wearing aurelian plates, spirit D axe, skull swords, wierd helmets, and one after one beat them in combat, each would have a different playstyle and attack style according to class.

now that would be an awsome quest, like lvl 15-20 quest.
