by Part-Time Modeler » Thu Oct 08, 2009 8:27 pm

Yes, your gold will be kept and xp will not change.
Penalty xp and an inventory wipe are the changes that will affect the players.

Penalty xp will be set to zero. So when the level cap is raised to 40 it wont take you forever to work off that insane amount of negative xp that has built up. For me I have -1.3 million xp, would take quite a while to get back to leveling.

The inventory has to be wiped because of weapon level adjustments. Some weapons that are in game now will be higher level weapons in Beta, some weapons will also be made more rare. The point of the weapon wipe is to set everyone back even, so a player that has a rare weapon in Pre-Beta will not be all powerful if that weapon is set to be a super rare weapon in Beta.
