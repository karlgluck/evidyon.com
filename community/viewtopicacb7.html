Something like that would require php scripting and databasing and just too much work for a small project like PV..

But as of the current map editor I find it very hard to imagine how we can work in a collaborative effort in making maps. Here's why: (a) Those working on adjacent locations will need to be aware of the exact tiles the other maps cut off at (ex. water lines), (b) theirs no GOOD way of determining your location on the map, sure you can mark every 10 tiles but without being able to zoom out its still very hard to have a sense of location, and (c) Even if we *can* combine our maps, how will it work if we need to add 100x100 from one map into a map that's say 200x200 being that all maps are saved at 300x300?

I started working on B1 and realized just how much work (or "additional" work, rather) there would need to be, and how good our communication would have to be, in order to make these maps work.
