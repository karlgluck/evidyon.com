by thadiusofx3 » Sat Dec 30, 2006 6:54 pm

Basic types of quests:

Delivery Quests: Deliver an item to a person.
Retrieval Quests: Retrieve an item from a person and bring it back to quest giver.
Theft Quests: Steal an item and bring it back to the quest giver.
Kill Quests: Kill an NPC or multiple NPC's.
Collection Quests: Collect Items off of NPC's (say 20 clubs or something) and bring them back to the quest giver.
