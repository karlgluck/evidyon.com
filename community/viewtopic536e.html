by marzuku » Wed Oct 28, 2009 2:18 pm

Well firstly i thought the game was about creating your own character by giving him personally designed stats so he/she is a unique build/character.
Requiering a level and on top of that a stats requierement totally slaughters that idea logically.
Ill explain: with this current system you get forced to put on stats where you dont want them this therfore makes you a character of what the creators decided not yourself the only customization will be like 1 or two stats difference.

Example of something that happened: lvled a caster named FrankTheTank and noticed at level 20 that i could not wield any wepons because i had to much intel compared to wisdom which i needed to wield the gear, i will therefore be forced to wait for reset or put all my stats in wisdom as i level to be able to wield wepons intended for my class.

Suggesting Class & level req, instead of the current system stat level req, or else nobody will truly be free to create own characters with different traits than others.
