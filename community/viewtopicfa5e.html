by thadiusofx3
Sorry i was on my way to my parents house.

Ok lets do a little test.

Open up blender so you have a fresh project.

There should be a cube in the middle of the screen.
Don't move it, rotate, nothing.

Ok now press f5 and it will take you to the material screen, and it should already have a material associated with it.
Now press f6 to go to the texture screen. Click add new, then under texture type hit image. Now go to load image, find the image you want and then hit select image.
Now, press F12 to render and it should show the cube with whatever texture you selected on it.
