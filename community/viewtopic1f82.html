by Karl G. » Sat May 16, 2009 10:33 am

We discussed lowering the numbers, but (1) higher numbers give more room for a variety of weapons and (2) as my brother pointed out, we don't want to move toward the Runescape system where level 70 characters do 4 damage. That's an extreme example, but I'd rather err on the side of numbers that are too big than ones that are too small.
