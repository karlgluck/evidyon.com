by Stick Stickly » Thu Nov 05, 2009 5:25 am

So far I really like it. I mean obviously there's a few graphic things that bother me but compared to xen graphics overall, they're realy nice. The combat is very smooth but I did notice when using whirlwind attack he didn't actually spin. But minor things like that doesnt make a game for me. I do think that party xp is a little hax but if I remember correctly that's going to be fixed. The game is lacking somethings such as houses and more merchents that normally make a town feel thriving and busy but unlike some others, I understand it's beta. (which is different from xen beta cause that games been in "beta" for almost 10 years>.>)I haven't played enough yet but so far it's looking great. Overall, I'm level 17 and really looking forward to updates. I can't believe three college kids put this together in their spare time. I already got another xenimus fan coming over to try Evidyon.

Also, some commands are dumb like /standup but I'm hoping those will be revised and changed.
