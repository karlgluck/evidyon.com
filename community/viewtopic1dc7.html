Ok I am willing to give whatever aid I can in this endeavor.
Quite a few people know me for ripping into EJ and trying to get his bum to do things.
I have always been for game balance and would love to help in that aspect mainly.


Is the game going to be more old school, or old school with better tweaks?

I would love old school with better tweaks.

For one I would love to see more weapons, that have varied requirements.
These would ensure no carbon copy builds.

For one, say a pair of nun-chuks (just and example) these would have great damage, but would need strength, agility and intelligence to properly weild.
On the other hand a Claymore would need mainly strength and cons.

This way people could make new characters based on a weapon they find = fewer perfect build characters.

Wisdom would not be good for weapon reqs, but would be a huge factor in skill points and magical spells.


In my opinion any class should be able to have any build they want, the stats should be there to maintain evenness.

I see no reason why Fighters can be either Balanced, Strength like Conan, Speed Like Zorro, Agility like a cat, or Intelligence to deal out deadly blows.

But that is my take, looking forward to hearing from you guys.
