I really liked the aurelian set you did, but this azure doesn't place a thought in my mind about how azure gear would look like...

The definition of azure:
1. of or having a light, purplish shade of blue, like that of a clear and unclouded sky.
2. Heraldry. of the tincture or color blue.

Mostly it's like a bit darker than sky blue.

For some reason I've pictured "Azure" to also have relevance to lightning... perhaps the shield could be round, with a darker blue rim, lighter blue texture towards the inside, with an even lighter blue animated lightning bolt (kind of how ominex was) on the middle of the shield. A ring around the lightning bolt of the same color of the bolt, with little curved spikes (not protruding) spiraling out just a slight bit from the middle ring, and the outer ring can have the same curved spikes spiraling toward the inside of the shield...

I'll see if I can draw up an example... if you guys don't mind I help with the ideas for the azure concept... for now here's a crap MSPaint example.

By the way... what's so special about the azure gear? Isn't it going to be extremely weak? Doesn't seem like a type of gear set to be really weak, especially if it only raises your mp or whatever.
