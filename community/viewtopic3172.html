idea for an arena server

by mr_slippyfist » Thu Sep 04, 2008 6:06 pm

So i have this idea for an arena server... it is somewhat like Nox..

It would be like Evidyon/Xen except without saved levels and gear.
When you log onto the server you create a character, choose race and class, distribute 100 stat points and log on.
The map would provide everything you need for PvP: gear, potions etc would spawn on the ground every so often.
Multiple maps would be cycled with different game modes such as capture the flag, team deathmatch etc.
No PvE/leveling just PvP for the hell of it!

Let me know what you think guys.

Also TO KARL: would this be easy to implement? i would be willing to help in making the maps and anything else it involves.
